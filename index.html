⚕ Nutrition Coaching

Your Maintenance Calories (TDEE)
💧
Hydration is key. Aim for 35ml per kg of body weight daily. If you're training hard, add extra 500ml for every hour of exercise.
🥗
Protein timing. Distribute 25-40g of protein across 4-5 meals. Your body absorbs it better and muscle protein synthesis stays elevated throughout the day.
⚡
Pre-workout carbs. Eating 1-2g of carbs per kg of body weight 1-2 hours before training gives you clean, sustained energy without bloating.
🍲
Cook at home. Restaurant meals average 200-400 extra kcal more than home-cooked equivalents. Batch cooking on Sundays is your secret weapon.
📈
Track consistently. Studies show people who log food daily for 12 weeks lose 10% more body fat than those who don't — even without changing what they eat.
🎬
Sleep & recovery. Less than 7 hours of sleep increases cortisol, tanks insulin sensitivity, and makes muscle repair nearly impossible. Prioritize rest.
